About Me


Back in 1978, I was an up and coming guitarist in the L.A. scene. However, a tragic thing happened: my hands began to shrink. Soon, they were too small to play the guitar. My hopes and dreams, gone. I was at rock bottom.


Then, while roaming through Toys R Us one day, I found my saving grace: a toy guitar. Since then, I've been melting faces all across the globe.

Work History

Lead Guitarist, The Muppets: shredded hard with Kermit, carried Miss Piggie's gear, drove the bus.
Lead Guitarist, Elm St. Elementary School Band: honed my mini guitar skills on the ruthless elementary school circuit .
Foot Model, Nike: sometimes you gotta adapt, baby!
Hand Model, Dove: back when I had normal-sized hands, I was one of the most sought-after hand models on the planet.
